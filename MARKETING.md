Eden changes that. It's a production-quality desktop AI assistant that runs 100% locally via Ollama. All processing happens on your Mac using the qwen2.5:7b model - no data ever leaves your machine. You get:

### 2-Minute Pitch

"The AI revolution has a dark side: every powerful AI assistant requires you to upload your data to the cloud. ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini - they all train on your conversations. Your private thoughts, your code, your creative work - all processed on someone else's servers.

Eden was built to solve this problem. It's a privacy-first desktop AI assistant inspired by Tony Stark's JARVIS, but designed for everyone.

**Here's how it works:**

First, it's 100% local. We use Ollama to run the qwen2.5:7b language model directly on your Mac. All AI processing happens in your computer's RAM - no internet required after initial setup. Your data literally cannot leave your machine because there's no code that sends it anywhere.

Second, it's genuinely powerful. The 7 billion parameter model responds in 2-4 seconds on Apple Silicon, supports Korean and English, generates code, analyzes your screen with computer vision, integrates with Git and files, and remembers past conversations through a local RAG system.

Third, it's completely customizable. Unlike one-size-fits-all cloud AIs, you control 10 personality parameters: formality, verbosity, humor, emoji usage, technical depth, empathy, and more. The AI learns from your thumbs-up and thumbs-down feedback, optimizing its behavior using gradient descent - all locally.

Fourth, it's free forever. No subscriptions, no trials, no paid tiers. MIT license, open source, community-driven. We don't pay for servers because you do the computing. We don't collect data because we can't access it.

**Who is this for?**

- Developers who value privacy and hate subscriptions
- Students on a budget who need AI help
- Professionals handling sensitive data (legal, medical, financial)
- Anyone tired of big tech surveillance

Eden proves you don't have to sacrifice privacy for AI power. Download it today and experience AI on your terms."

## Press Release

### FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

**Eden: Open-Source, Privacy-First AI Assistant Launches - No Cloud, No Subscriptions, No Compromises**

_Free Desktop Application Brings JARVIS-like AI to Everyone While Keeping Data 100% Local_

**Seoul, South Korea - January 16, 2025** - Today marks the launch of Eden, a production-quality desktop AI assistant that runs entirely on users' computers. Unlike cloud-based alternatives like ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini, Eden processes all data locally, ensuring complete privacy while eliminating subscription fees.

"The AI industry has conditioned users to accept a false choice between power and privacy," says Matthew, creator of Eden. "We built this to prove you can have both. Your conversations, your code, your creative work - it all stays on your machine, forever."

**Key Features:**

- **100% Local Processing**: All AI runs via Ollama using qwen2.5:7b model (7B parameters)
- **Zero Subscription Fees**: Free forever, MIT open-source license
- **Complete Privacy**: No telemetry, no tracking, no cloud storage
- **Offline Capability**: Works without internet after initial setup
- **Customizable Personality**: 10 adjustable parameters with learning system
- **Voice Integration**: Speech-to-text and text-to-speech in Korean and English
- **Screen Context**: LLaVA-powered computer vision for screen analysis
- **Developer Tools**: Git integration, file operations, code generation

**Technical Specifications:**

Eden is built with Tauri 2.9 (Rust + React), producing a lightweight 7.1MB application - 97% smaller than equivalent Electron builds. The qwen2.5:7b language model provides responses in 2-4 seconds on Apple Silicon Macs with Metal acceleration.

The application includes a unique 10-parameter persona system, allowing users to customize formality, verbosity, humor, emoji usage, and six other behavioral traits. A gradient-based learning system optimizes these parameters based on user feedback, all processed locally.

**Availability:**

Eden is available now for macOS (Intel and Apple Silicon) as a free download from GitHub. Windows support is planned for Q2 2025. The application requires macOS 11 or later, 16GB RAM minimum (24GB recommended), and 20GB free disk space for AI models.

**System Requirements:**

- Intel or Apple Silicon processor

Free forever. No trials, no paid tiers, no subscriptions.

## FAQ for Media

**Q: Why create another local LLM tool when Ollama exists?**
A: Ollama is CLI-only. We built a full GUI with voice, vision, persona customization, and learning - features you don't get from command-line tools.

**Q: How do you make money if it's free?**
A: We don't. This is a passion project to prove privacy-first AI is possible. No servers = no costs.

**Q: Is 7B good enough compared to GPT-4?**
A: For most tasks, yes. It's not as capable as 100B+ models, but it's private, fast, and free. Trade-offs are worth it for many users.

**Q: Why Tauri instead of Electron?**
A: 7.1MB vs 285MB builds. Rust security. Better performance. Modern architecture.

**Q: Windows support?**
A: Q2 2025. macOS first because that's where our users are (developers, creatives).

**Q: Can you really customize AI personality?**
A: Yes, 10 sliders control formality, verbosity, humor, etc. Learning system optimizes based on feedback. Unique to Eden.
